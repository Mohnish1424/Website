01 About

Driven by one
question — why?

Why do some organizations thrive while others collapse with seemingly equal resources? That question has defined my intellectual path and shaped every decision I've made.

It pushed me toward a dual academic track — BBA+MBA at Woxsen University paired with a BA in Political Science. Business shows you how organizations function. Political Science reveals why people and systems behave the way they do. Together, they sharpen every lens you bring to a problem.

I've served as Class Representative, Head Boy, and now President of E-Cell. Each role taught me a different version of the same truth: execution is where most ideas die, and leadership is the art of closing that gap.

My approach is simple — learn something, apply it, improve, repeat.
